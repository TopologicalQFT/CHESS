# Queen's Gambit Declined (as Black)

Your defense to 1. d4:

```
1. d4 d5 2. c4 e6 3. Nc3 Nf6 4. Bg5 Be7 5. e3 O-O
```

**The plan:** the e6+d5 pawn triangle is a fortress. Develop calmly, then free your game with ...c5 or ...dxc4 followed by ...b5/...Bb7.

**Key points:**
- Don't try to keep a grabbed c4 pawn — return it for development. Greed loses here.
- Your problem piece is the c8-bishop; plan ...b6+...Bb7 or ...dxc4+...b5+...Bb7 to give it a life.
- Watch the [[Opening Traps]] note: the Elephant Trap punishes White's greedy Nxd5 — know it from BOTH sides.
- The ...Nbd7 move order can look like it hangs d5 — it doesn't (that's the trap).
