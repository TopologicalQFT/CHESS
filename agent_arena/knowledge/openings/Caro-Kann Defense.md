# Caro-Kann Defense (facing it as White)

After 1. e4 c6 — the Exchange variation keeps it simple:

```
1. e4 c6 2. d4 d5 3. exd5 cxd5 4. Bd3 Nc6 5. c3
```

**The plan:** symmetrical structure, no theory battles. Develop (Nf3 or Ne2, Bf4, O-O), play for small edges: better minor pieces, the e5 outpost, a possible minority attack later (b4–b5 against their queenside).

**Key points:**
- Bd3 before Nf3 stops Black's easy ...Bg4 pin.
- The position rewards patience — improve your worst piece (see [[Move Selection Checklist]]) rather than forcing things.
