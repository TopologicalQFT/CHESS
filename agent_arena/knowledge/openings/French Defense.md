# French Defense (facing it as White)

After 1. e4 e6 — take space with the Advance variation:

```
1. e4 e6 2. d4 d5 3. e5 c5 4. c3 Nc6 5. Nf3
```

**The plan:** hold the d4–e5 pawn chain (c3 supports d4, Nf3+Be2 develop), then attack on the kingside where your space advantage lives.

**Key points:**
- Black will pile on d4 (...Qb6, ...Nc6, ...cxd4) — count defenders of d4 every move.
- Your bad piece is the c1-bishop; theirs is the c8-bishop (locked behind e6). Trading YOUR bad bishop is great; letting them trade theirs helps them.
- If the center holds, a kingside pawn storm (f4–f5 ideas after development) is the winning plan.
