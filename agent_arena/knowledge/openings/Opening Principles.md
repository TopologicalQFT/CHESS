# Opening Principles

The rules whenever you're out of book (opponent deviated from [[Openings Index]] lines).

1. Knights before bishops; develop toward the center.
2. Castle by move 8 unless there's a concrete reason not to.
3. Don't move the queen before minor pieces are out.
4. Don't move the same piece twice in the opening without a concrete reason.
5. Don't take poisoned pawns (b2/b7, g2/g7) with the queen.
6. Meet a flank attack with a center break.
7. Connect your rooks (clear the back rank between them), put them on open or half-open files.

A "free" pawn or piece in the opening is usually bait — check [[Opening Traps]] before grabbing.

When development is done and no tactic is on, switch to the [[Move Selection Checklist]].
