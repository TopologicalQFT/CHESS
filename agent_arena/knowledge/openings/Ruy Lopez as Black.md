# Ruy Lopez as Black (Spanish)

When White plays 3. Bb5 instead of the Italian's 3. Bc4 — the Morphy Defense is solid:

```
1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bb5 a6 4. Ba4 Nf6 5. O-O Be7
```

**The plan:** ...b5 (kicking the bishop), ...d6, ...O-O, then maneuver: ...Re8, ...Bf8 (the classic regrouping). Slow game, totally sound.

**Key points:**
- Don't fear 4. Bxc6 dxc6 5. Nxe5 — ...Qd4! forks e5 and e4 and regains the pawn immediately.
- After ...b5+...c4 ideas, White's a4-bishop can get trapped — that's the Noah's Ark trap working in YOUR favor (see [[Opening Traps]]).
- Don't play ...exd4 too early; keep the strong e5 pawn.
