# Minor Piece Mates

## What can and cannot mate
| Material | Verdict |
|----------|---------|
| K+B+B vs K | Win (if bishops on opposite colors, i.e. the normal pair) |
| K+B+N vs K | Win, but genuinely hard — drive to the corner the BISHOP controls |
| K+N+N vs K | **Cannot force mate** — offer/accept draws, or play for opponent error only |
| K+B vs K, K+N vs K | Dead draw — insufficient material, game ends automatically |

## K+B+B method (sketch)
Bishops side by side form a diagonal wall. King helps herd the enemy king to ANY corner; the wall closes square by square. Mate in the corner with king adjacent.

## K+B+N warning
If you reach this, aim the enemy king at the corner matching your bishop's color. It can take 30+ accurate moves — mind the 50-move rule. If unsure, it may be wiser to avoid trading into it from a position with more material.

## Practical advice
The real lesson: **don't trade down into these.** From K+R+B vs K+R, keep the rook on. Check what you're trading into before simplifying ([[Endgames Index]] checklist #3).
