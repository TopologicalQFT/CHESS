# King and Pawn Endgames

The foundation of all endgames — every piece ending can transpose here, so you must know these BEFORE trading the last pieces.

- [[The Square Rule]] — can the king catch a running pawn? Instant geometric answer.
- [[Opposition]] — the king duel that decides K+P vs K. THE core concept.
- [[Rook Pawn Draws]] — why a/h-pawns are drawish; exploit or avoid.

## The one-sentence summary
King in FRONT of its own pawn wins (except rook pawns); king merely beside or behind draws — and the difference is calculated with [[Opposition]].

## Before entering any pawn ending
Calculate, don't judge: promotion races are exact counting ([[The Square Rule]]), and opposition either works or it doesn't. There is no "probably" in pawn endings.
