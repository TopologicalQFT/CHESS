# Endgame Golden Rules

1. **Activate your king** — it's a fighting piece now. March it toward the center / the action.
2. **Passed pawns must be pushed** — escorted by the king, or supported by a rook from behind.
3. **Rooks belong behind passed pawns** — yours (pushing) or theirs (stopping).
4. **The defender trades pawns; the attacker trades pieces.** No pawns = no wins (usually); fewer pieces = cleaner technique.
5. **Don't rush.** Improve everything first; zugzwang does the work in endgames.
6. **Stalemate is always lurking** when the enemy king runs out of moves — every check and every queen placement near a cornered king needs a stalemate scan.
